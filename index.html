About Us

Future for All (FFA) is a non-profit organization founded by young Vietnamese professionals born in Vietnam and Germany, currently studying and working in Germany, Australia, and Vietnam. Registered as a non-profit association in Frankfurt, Germany (Registration number: VR 17294), FFA is driven by a strong desire to reconnect with our homeland and contribute to community development.

Our members bring diverse expertise in fields such as accounting, finance, social work, marketing, and communication, alongside years of volunteer experience with governmental and non-governmental organizations both in Vietnam and abroad. Leveraging this knowledge and experience, FFA carries out meaningful projects aimed at building a sustainable society, with a particular focus on improving opportunities for disadvantaged children in Vietnam.

Sponsorship Program Details

There are four steps to sponsor:

  1. If you are interested in the sponsorship program, please contact us via Instagram, Facebook or email for further information.
  2. We will send you our program brochure and a list of frequently asked questions, and offer a one-on-one conversation, either in person or online, to discuss our organization and projects in more detail
  3. If you decide to join, we will provide the agreement along with details about a child.
  4. Once you sign the agreement, you will become a sponsor in the scholarship program.

Sponsorship is open to everyone‚Äîindividuals, families, companies, and organizations are all welcome to participate. You can choose to sponsor one child, or more than one; you and your friends can also sponsor a child as a group, based on your capability.

Children are selected for the program based on their financial need and academic potential, with guidance from our local partners, including schools and the Youth Union which have deep insight into the children‚Äôs circumstances and the local community. Additionally, parents must commit to ensuring their child completes their education, signing an agreement that the child will not drop out of school midway to maintain eligibility for the program.

Ending Sponsorship

If you need to end your sponsorship, please notify us via email at least one month in advance. We will work to find another sponsor to ensure the child's education continues without interruption.

If the child graduates or no longer needs support, we will inform you via email and provide the option to sponsor another child or end the sponsorship.
