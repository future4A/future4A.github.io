About Us
Future for All is a non-profit organization established by young Vietnamese individuals (born in Vietnam and Germany) who are studying and working in Germany, Australia, Vietnam. The organization is registered as a non-profit association in Frankfurt, Germany (Registration number: VR 17294). With great enthusiasm and compassion, we wish to connect with our homeland - Vietnam and contribute to community development. FFA members are young and experienced professionals with Vietnamese heritage in various fields such as accounting, finance, social work, marketing, and communication.
Additionally, we have many years of volunteer experience in governmental and non-governmental organizations in Vietnam and abroad. With valuable knowledge and skills, we hope to carry out meaningful projects for sustainable society development, particularly for disadvantaged children in Vietnam.
